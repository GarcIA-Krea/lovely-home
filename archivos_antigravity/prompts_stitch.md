# Prompts Listos para Google Stitch (Adaptados a Lovely Home Real)

Tras analizar el logo oficial (verde neón suave/lima y rosa) y las fotos reales de los apartamentos (que destacan por su madera clara, acabados modernos, paredes blancas muy limpias, y toques rústicos/cálidos como el ladrillo expuesto y baldosas decorativas), he adaptado profundamente los prompts. 

Para lograr la mejor fusión entre la estética premium de *Outsite* y el espíritu cálido de *Lovely Home*, **utilizaremos el modo oscuro (Dark Mode)**, ya que el logo verde neón resaltará maravillosamente sobre fondos oscuros, y las fotos iluminadas y cálidas de los apartamentos generarán un contraste espectacular.

Aquí tienes los prompts refinados. Te recomiendo introducirlos **uno a la vez** en Stitch.

---

### Prompt 1: El Inicio (Hero Section)

> **Prompt:** Create a premium, ultra-modern "Hero Section" for a short-term rental platform called "Lovely Home". The aesthetic must be extremely high-end and deeply inspired by outsite.co. Use a very sleek dark mode theme (background #121212). The typography must be a premium geometric sans-serif font like 'Inter' or 'Plus Jakarta Sans'. Use a large, edge-to-edge photograph of a bright, modern apartment interior with light wood accents and clean white walls as the background image (the photography should contrast beautifully with the dark UI). Include a bold main headline: "Tu Hogar en Medellín", a subheadline: "Cerca de todo lo que necesitas", and a primary call-to-action button saying "Explorar Alojamientos". The button should be accented in a soft neon green/lime color (like #C5E16A) based on the brand logo, with a subtle glassmorphism hover effect. Include a sleek top navigation bar.

### Prompt 2: Tarjetas de Propiedades (Property Cards)
*(Pégalo solo cuando estés feliz con el Prompt 1 en Stitch)*

> **Prompt:** Keeping exactly the same dark mode aesthetic, typography, and neon green accent color from the previous design, create a premium "Property Listing Card" component. The card should have a very dark, sleek background (#1A1A1A) with soft, rounded corners. The top 70% of the card must be a beautiful, high-resolution edge-to-edge image of a modern apartment that features warm wood textures and exposed brick. Below the image, lay out the details: Property Title (e.g., "Provenza Modern Loft"), Location ("El Poblado, Medellín"), and a bold Price per night. Include 3 small elegant minimalist icons for amenities: Fast Wifi, Dedicated Workspace, and Flexible Check-in. When hovered, the card should have a very smooth, subtle lift animation with a soft glow to feel premium and alive.

### Prompt 3: Sección de Comunidad y "Prueba Social"
*(Pégalo solo cuando el Prompt 2 luzce perfecto)*

> **Prompt:** Continuing with our sleek dark mode design system, create a "Social Proof / Community Testimonial" section. The section headline should read "Lo Que Nuestros Huéspedes Dicen". Design a grid of 3 testimonial cards. Each card should feature a high-quality portrait photo of the guest, their name, their home city, and their quote about staying at Lovely Home. The design must feel editorial and use generous padding so the text is highly readable against the dark background. Use the neon green/lime brand color subtly for quotation marks or highlight accents.
